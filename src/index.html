Spiritual Enlightenment : About Us

This website was founded by an ex-Christian named Abigail. Having always been a spiritual person, Abigail was raised as a devout Catholic. While her spirituality remained consistent, she always found herself at odds with the beliefs of her church, butting heads with nuns and questioning her priests. Feeling a calling that she had always known but never understood, she took a hike into the woods, just listening to the wind and the leaves. That ever-present calling seemed to always be at its loudest whilst outdoors. She talked to the trees (literally!), she followed the river, and suddenly, she heard a voice. Not male, female. "The earth is our mother, we must take care of her, " the voice sang, an enchanting tune. Running into the woods, following the voice, and finally, she came across a woman standing next to a deer, only for the woman to fade away into mist. Upon returning home, Abigail looked into what this sign meant, stumbling across the mythology of the goddess of the hunt, Artemis. She told her church about this sign, still believing it to be a sign of God, believing the female voice was that of the virgin Mary. Once hearing about this sign, her priest told her to pray for forgiveness for her devilish sign. Confused, but determined, Abigail, then realized her faith no longer laid with the church. She delved into the religions of nature, Paganism, Wicca, and eventually witchcraft itself. Wanting to be the guiding light to others, she started this website, offering advice, information, and materials to help those find their ever-present calling!
